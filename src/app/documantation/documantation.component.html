Introduction

With Digiproof, you can timestamp your web content on polygon blockchain from the comfort of your site. No prior blockchain experience necessary. After the set-up, everything is taken care of automatically!

Digiproof creates a unique and universal fingerprint (the ‘hash’) for all your posts, pages and media files. If the input changes, the hash becomes totally different.

This hash is added to the blockchain with a date and time. Because you (the website owner) have the input that results in this specific hash, you can prove that you published the content at that point in time.

Javasctipt SDK

Javascript SDK help you to implement the frontend popup, allow visitors to view the profstamped details on blockchain for that article.

To implement the SDK just add the digiproof SDK into your webpage

         
            <script src="https://domain/digiproof.min.js">

Then add the digiproof tag just before your </body> tag of the webpage or at the bottom of the webpage

         
         <digi-poroof key="api key" webkey="website key">
